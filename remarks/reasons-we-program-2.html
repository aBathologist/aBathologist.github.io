What is Programming?

Computation is the autonomous part of inscription; it is the immanent self-determination of writing. We seize on computation when we confine writing to an exact description of what and where it is itself is inscribed. Computing is the writing that reads and rewrites itself. The computer is the agent who mediates this autonomous developing.

The programmatic is that which can be written out in advance. A program sets out in writing what is to be done. Executing a program means reading what was written and enacting the plan described. Computation is where the programmatic comes into what is essentially its own: what was written in advance users a computer to read and execute itself.
